Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my water monitoring device? ‚ñº

Setting up your device is simple:

  1. Install the KD Water Solution app and create an account
  2. Follow the in-app setup wizard
  3. Connect your device to Wi-Fi using the app
  4. Install the device at your water source following the included instructions
  5. Complete the calibration process through the app

The entire process typically takes 10-15 minutes.

What water parameters does the app monitor? ‚ñº

Our system monitors key water quality parameters including:

  • pH levels (acidity/alkalinity)
  • Total Dissolved Solids (TDS)
  • Chlorine levels
  • Temperature
  • Turbidity (water clarity)
  • Electrical conductivity
  • Optional: Heavy metals and bacteria (with premium sensors)
How often should I replace my water filters? ‚ñº

Filter replacement depends on your water usage and quality:

  • Standard filters: Every 3-6 months
  • Carbon filters: Every 6-12 months
  • Reverse osmosis membranes: Every 12-24 months

The app will send you personalized reminders based on your actual usage and water quality data.

Why am I getting water quality alerts? ‚ñº

Water quality alerts are triggered when:

  • Parameters exceed safe drinking water standards
  • Sudden changes in water quality are detected
  • Filter replacement is overdue
  • Device maintenance is required
  • System detects potential contamination

Always take water quality alerts seriously and follow the recommended actions in the app.

Can I use the app without an internet connection? ‚ñº

The app has limited offline functionality:

  • View previously downloaded water quality data
  • Access offline help and troubleshooting guides
  • Record manual water test results

However, real-time monitoring, alerts, and data synchronization require an internet connection.

How do I export my water quality data? ‚ñº

To export your data:

  1. Open the app and go to "Reports" section
  2. Select the date range you want to export
  3. Choose your preferred format (PDF, CSV, or Excel)
  4. Tap "Export" and select where to save or share the file

Premium users can set up automatic monthly reports via email.
